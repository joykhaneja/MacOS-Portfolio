About Me

Hi, I'm Joy Khaneja

I’m a cybersecurity enthusiast with a deep interest in threat intelligence, digital forensics, OSINT, and offensive security. My hands-on experience spans projects and internships involving real-world investigations, threat detection, and secure network automation.

I’m especially drawn to the intersection of cybercrime and investigation.. from tracking dark web activity to building tools that support analysis and incident response. I enjoy working where technology meets critical thinking.

Beyond cybersecurity, I’m passionate about fields like quantum mechanics, geopolitics, space tech, and military history.. exploring how systems, strategy, and science shape our world. I also love literature, philosophy, psychology, and Formula 1.

Outside of tech, I explore photography, road trips, and gaming. I believe in blending curiosity with action.. staying creative, analytical, and always learning.
